FAQ's
Membership & Access ▼

How many members are there in the club?
The club is limited to 25 Founding Members and 125 General Members to ensure exclusivity.

How long does the membership process take?
The average waiting time is 30 to 45 days.

How will I know if I’ve been accepted or denied?
You will be contacted via email with the final decision.

Can I transfer my membership?
No, memberships are non-transferable.

What if I cancel my membership?
Please check the membership bylaws for details.

Car Ownership, Logistics & Storage ▼

Can I own my own car?
Yes, most members have their private collection.

What is the benefit of buying a car through ThreeSixty?
Vehicles will be registered in Andorra, offering savings of 30-35%.

Where will my car be stored?
Members can store their vehicles in Andorra or Barcelona.

Driving Experiences & Track Days ▼

How often can I participate in track days?
Members receive a calendar with 6 pre-organized F1 track days per year.

What racing circuits does ThreeSixty have access to?
We have access to almost all F1 circuits worldwide.

Do I need prior track experience?
No. The club provides professional coaching and training programs.

Exclusive Experiences & Travel ▼

How many activities can I do in the club?
Members can participate in unlimited activities.

Can I organize my own activities?
Yes, members can request customized experiences.

Payments & Costs ▼

What are the general costs involved?
Founder Members have zero annual costs; other expenses depend on usage.

How do I pay for additional services?
The club operates on a prepaid credit system.

Residences & Lifestyle ▼

What are ThreeSixty’s private residences?
The club owns luxury villas and apartments for members.

Can I use club properties for business trips?
Yes, they can be booked for personal or business use.

F1 & Motorsports Access ▼

Can I attend Formula 1 races with the club?
Yes, members have VIP access to F1 events.

Can I meet F1 drivers?
Yes, members enjoy exclusive behind-the-scenes access.
